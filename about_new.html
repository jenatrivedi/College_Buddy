The goal of the College Buddy is therefore to provide access to free, high-quality, early grade reading resources that use and understand.

INTRODUCTION

Our team started College buddy because there is no connection between the seniors and juniors in our college. It happens that the junior don’t know who are the seniors and sometime because of this issue juniors can’t get notes and not get informative with the college. And the seniors also can’t share their notes and instruments to their juniors. That's why we have started the college buddy application which is web application which can become the medium between juniors and seniors.

"WE ARE NOT A TEAM BEACUSE WE WORK TOGETHER WE ARE TEAM BEACUSE WE RESPECT TRUST AND CARE FOR EACHOTHER." By this sentence we have made this web application to make an interactive platform.

COLLEGE BUDDY is the project to develop a framework of virtual repository of learning resources with a single-window search facility. Filtered and federated searching is employed to facilitate focused searching so that learners can find out the right resource with least effort and in minimum time. COLLEGE BUDDY is designed to hold content of notes and provides interface support. It is designed to provide support for all academic levels including researchers and life-long learners, all disciplines, all popular forms of access devices and differently-abled learners. It is being developed to help students to prepare for entrance and competitive examinations, to enable people to learn and prepare. It is being developed at L.J. Institute of Technology Ahmedabad.

WHO IS BEHIND THE COLLEGE BUDDY?

The COLLEGE BUDDY -platform is a collaborative endeavor which will require involvement from a broad spectrum of stakeholders in order to be truly successful and widely used. It is being built based on existing quality learning resources provided from a variety of initiatives. The content pool will expand over time through discovery and sharing of more existing quality content, translations and localizations of the platform’s content, as well as additions of newly created content. The Platform will be designed both for direct use by a variety of user groups and for integration with existing initiatives in the field.

The COLLEGE BUDDY, an international effort involving multiple stakeholders working to transform book development, procurement and distribution to ensure that no student is without notes and instruments. The mission of the COLLEGE BUDDY is to guarantee that student of the college have the books and learning materials they need to learn to read and read to learn.

WHAT IS THE PURPOSE OF THE COLLEGE BUDDY?

The COLLEGE BUDDY collects existing high quality open educational reading resources, and makes them available on web, mobile and destop. The COLLEGE BUDDY currently offers resources of college notes, instruments and by end 2020 the goal is to provide some additional features like resources of Novels.

The platform also facilitates translation and localization of COLLEGE BUDDY -resources to more than 50 notes and instruments.
